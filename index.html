Our Vision

To provide quality education that builds confident, self-motivated, and responsible citizens capable of contributing to society.

Our Mission

To deliver holistic education through effective teaching methods, moral values, and a nurturing environment from nursery through 9th grade.

Our History

Tyneceploh Educational Foundation was established in 2018 with the goal of offering accessible and quality education to children in the Rehab community. Over the years, the school has grown significantly in size and impact, fostering academic excellence and social responsibility.
